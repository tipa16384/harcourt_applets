Investigating Tangents
Instructions:
  • Click on a button and choose a curve to investigate.
  • On the curve, choose a point P at x = -1, -0.5, 0, 0.5, or 1.
  • Move your mouse over the graph and a line will appear; click when you think you have found the tangent line at P. Zoom in on the line to look more closely at P as you experiment trying to find the tangent. What is your estimate for the slope of the tangent? Experiment with other points and the other curves.
